TWICE Members Profile

Jihyo

Park Ji-hyo (Korean: 박지효; born Park Ji-soo on February 1, 1997), known mononymously as Jihyo, is a South Korean singer. She is the leader and vocalist of the South Korean girl group Twice formed by JYP Entertainment.

Stage Name: Jihyo (지효)

Birth Name: Park Ji Soo (박지수) but legalized her name to Park Ji Hyo (박지효)

Nationality: Korean

Birthday: February 1, 1997

Zodiac Sign: Aquarius

Position: Leader, Main Vocalist

Height: 162 cm (5 ft 3¾ in)

Weight: 56 kg (123 lbs)

Blood Type: O

Instagram: _zyozyo

Facts

Jihyo Facts

- She was born in Guri, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea.

- Jihyo’s real name is Park Jisoo. She legalized her name to Park Jihyo right before Sixteen.

- She became a JYP trainee when she was a third grader after she won the 2nd place in a contest on Junior Naver for a child role.

- She is the Twice member who trained the most. She trained for 10 years.

- She was voted leader by the members and not JYP himself (by anonymous voting).

- Her representative color is Apricot.

- Jihyo is ranked 79th on TC Candler The most beautiful faces of of 2020.
