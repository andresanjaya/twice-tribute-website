About TWICE

TWICE (트와이스) is a girl group consisting of 9 members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. The group debuted on October 20, 2015, through the survival show Sixteen, under JYP Entertainment. As of February 2020, JYPE is partnering with Republic Records to help promote TWICE worldwide.

TWICE Members Profile

Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu.

Nayeon

Im Na-yeon (Korean: 임나연; born September 22, 1995), known mononymously as Nayeon, is a South Korean singer. After participating on the reality survival television show Sixteen in 2015, she became a member in the South Korean girl group Twice formed by JYP Entertainment

Jeongyeon

Yoo Jeong-yeon (Korean: 유정연; born 1 November 1996), known mononymously as Jeongyeon (Korean: 정연), is a South Korean singer. She is a member of Twice, a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment.

Momo

Hirai Momo (Japanese: 平井 もも, born November 9, 1996), known mononymously as Momo (Korean: 모모; Japanese: モモ), is a Japanese singer, rapper and dancer based in South Korea. She is a member of the South Korean girl group Twice, formed by JYP Entertainment.

Sana

Minatozaki Sana (Japanese: 湊崎 紗夏, born December 29, 1996), known mononymously as Sana (Korean: 사나; Japanese: サナ), is a Japanese singer based in South Korea. She is a member of the K-pop girl group Twice, formed by JYP Entertainment.

Jihyo

Park Ji-hyo (Korean: 박지효; born Park Ji-soo on February 1, 1997), known mononymously as Jihyo, is a South Korean singer. She is the leader and vocalist of the South Korean girl group Twice formed by JYP Entertainment.

Mina

Myoui Mina (Japanese: 名井 南, born March 24, 1997), known mononymously as Mina (Korean: 미나; Japanese: ミナ), is an American-born Japanese singer and dancer based in South Korea. She is a member of the girl group Twice, formed by JYP Entertainment.

Dahyun

Kim Da-hyun (Korean: 김다현; born May 28, 1998), known mononymously as Dahyun, is a South Korean singer and rapper. She is a member of the South Korean girl group Twice formed by JYP Entertainment.

Chaeyoung

Son Chae-young (Korean: 손채영; born April 23, 1999), known mononymously as Chaeyoung, is a South Korean rapper and singer. She is a member of the South Korean girl group Twice, formed by JYP Entertainment.

Tzuyu

Chou Tzu-yu (Chinese: 周子瑜, born 14 June 1999), known mononymously as Tzuyu (Korean: 쯔위, is a Taiwanese singer based in South Korea. She is the only Taiwanese member of the girl group Twice, formed by JYP Entertainment.
